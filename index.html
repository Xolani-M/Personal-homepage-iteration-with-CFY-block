Manifestation

The Manifestation of all that is in me.



Image by:unsplash.com


Growing up as a little boy, you do not really know or understand much, but what is mostly clear even in that young age is what you desire to become and what you want out of life. One of the most exciting things i enjoyed as a kid was indulging in the idea of growing up to be a writer, to create and to build stuff. With the oblivion of rejection, limitations and fear, everything i desired seemed within reach. Well it was until i affiliated myself with the world and met cruelty, disaster and disappointment.

What i am most grateful for is that through all the different phases i went through in life my desires and vision never faded away... faith kept me going and along the way i picked up some virtues, courage, and a purpose. Learning for me became an essential skill that helped me through difficult situations, without the knowledge acquired i would'nt be the person that i am today, hence the saying goes 'The knowledge of the truth shall set you free'. In my journey i became a student of many things including computer programming. Now this is where i strongly believe is the beginning of great things, considering that we now live in a digital age. I personally have found a space where my inner-child can thrive and engineer beautiful things that could potentially transform people's lives and possibly reach places beyond my comprehension. One of the things i long for is to be in a thriving environment, one that will constantly inspire growth and challenge me to be a better person each and every day.

What i am realising now as a young adult is that we as people sometimes waste too much time trying to fit in our societies and end up losing ourselves in the process, instead of investing that very same time in our personal growth and in building our craft. Some people are fortunate enough to find and learn to forgive themselves in a very young age, in the process of that enlightenment they become reunited with their inner-child while they still have the time and energy to flourish and witness the manifestation of all that is in them and ultimately fulfil their purpose. But we don't all walk in the same shoes and that's okay... pursue rest, healing and self-awareness, find your way back to your most peaceful state of mind, if you've never experience that create a new reality for yourself, stop procrastinating, put in the time, persevere even in failure because trust me you will fail, sometimes more than once but you need to keep going because your well-being depends on it.

In conclusion i strongly believe that the manifestation of all that is in you as a person does not necessarily mean it's all about you or your family, but it's about your community, your generation and the world at large. Great enlightenment comes with great responsibility and that responsibility gives you a sense of purpose which will later lead you to a happy and satisfying life... so whoever you are, wherever you are, spread your light and shine bright, give service to your generation and inspire the younger ones to be better people and teach them to lead a purpose driven life.
